ℹ️ About ReflectAI

ReflectAI is an AI-powered journaling companion designed to help you understand your emotional patterns and practice self-reflection.

🌟 Key Features

🧠 Empathetic Reflections
AI-generated personalized reflections based on your emotional state
💬 Context-Aware Questions
Follow-up questions tailored to your specific situation
📊 Detailed Analytics
Emotional analytics and pattern recognition over time
🔍 Full-Text Search
Search and filter across all your journal entries
🌿 Coping Suggestions
Grounding techniques and coping strategies when needed
🚨 Crisis Detection
Automatic detection with professional support resources

🤖 How It Works

  1. Write your thoughts freely in the journal
  2. ReflectAI analyzes your emotion and sentiment
  3. You receive an empathetic reflection with actionable insights
  4. Your entry is saved and analyzed for patterns over time
⚠️ Important Notice

This is NOT a substitute for professional therapy.
If you're in crisis, please contact a mental health professional immediately.
Your data is stored locally and encrypted on the server.

🛠️ Technologies Used

  • Flask - Web framework
  • Google Gemini API - AI responses
  • Transformers - Emotion detection
  • TextBlob - Sentiment analysis
  • SQLite - Local data storage
  • Chart.js - Data visualization
  • Bootstrap 5 - UI framework
  • Scikit-learn - Pattern analysis

💡 Tips for Better Insights

  • Journal regularly (3-5 times per week is ideal)
  • Be honest and specific about your feelings
  • Track patterns by searching past entries
  • Review your analytics weekly to spot trends
  • Use the insights to inform your self-care
